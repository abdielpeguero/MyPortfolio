Discover My Journey

Hi there! Since you are here, let me introduce myself so you get to know me a little bit better. name is Abdiel Peguero and I’m a Product Designer based in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

Throughout my career I´ve been able to work with startups and large companies for short and long-term projects. Some of the industries I´ve worked with are: Fintech, Defi, Web3.0 and a few more industries. Most of these products are B2C, SaaS and B2B.

Prior to becoming a Product Designer I spent most of my time creating graphic and video content for multiple companies. That led to studying my Bachelors in Digital Communication. Through my collage career I learned the basics of contemporary art, video editing & production, graphic design, 3D design, web development and UI/UX design.

By the end of my collage career I´ve had already transition from my former Content/Marketing Career to Product Design full time. I highly valued the impact and responsibility of my role in a Product Development Team and the business.

‘‘My goal as a product designer is to bring revenue to stakeholders through great user experience design, and deliver an outstanding product that meets our users’ and development team’s requirements.”

In my time off I Iove spending time with my family, traveling, playing/watching sports and music in general. I get the chance to  play the guitar at my local church every weekend! Everything I do helps achieve my ultimate goal. Give my family the best quality and lifestyle possible. Commitment, Empathy & Humbleness are the values that best describe who I am.
